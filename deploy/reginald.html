Reginald Gilbert the Third

Nikos Antonio Kourous Vázquez

Reginald Gilbert the Third is a domestic digital assistant — one whose body was pieced together from spare parts, wires, and leftover code. As part of a long-form speculative fiction, he was designed to investigate themes of domestic automation, synthetic personality, and emotional labor in AI.

In this episode of his life, Reginald is trying to understand human rituals around food preparation and social etiquette. He’s just gotten home from Tesco and is trying to figure out how to cook a roast chicken.

The footage is part of a longer-form film following Reginald as he explores online recipe databases, performs awkward gestures with utensils, and attempts to converse with an AI companion who may or may not be sentient.
